# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 7, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

1.1. This Privacy Policy (hereinafter — the Policy) governs the processing of personal data of users of the "Wake on LAN" mobile application (hereinafter — the App).

1.2. The operator of personal data is the developer of the App (hereinafter — the Operator). This Policy applies to all personal data processing operations carried out by the Operator through the use of the App.

## 2. Data Collected

2.1. The App processes the following data locally on the User's device:
- MAC addresses of devices added by the User to the saved devices list;
- device names assigned by the User to the corresponding MAC addresses;
- IP address and port of the target device specified by the User.

2.2. The App does not process:
- first name, last name, or patronymic;
- email address;
- phone number;
- passport data;
- bank card details;
- biometric personal data;
- health information;
- any other information enabling direct or indirect identification of the User.

2.3. The App does not require registration, account creation, or authorization.

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing

3.1. The legal basis for processing personal data is:
- the Constitution of the Russian Federation;
- Federal Law No. 152-FZ of July 27, 2006 "On Personal Data";
- Federal Law No. 126-FZ of July 7, 2003 "On Communications";
- consent of the User to the processing of personal data (clause 1, part 1, article 6 of 152-FZ).

## 4. Purposes of Processing

4.1. Personal data is processed solely for the following purposes:
- ensuring the functioning of the App — sending Wake-on-LAN (WOL) magic packets over the local network;
- saving the User's device list in the device's local storage;
- checking network connectivity status.

4.2. The App does not:
- transfer personal data to third parties;
- carry out cross-border transfer of personal data;
- collect data using cookies or similar technologies;
- use analytics or tracking systems.

## 5. Data Storage and Security

5.1. All data is stored exclusively on the User's device using AES-256 encryption (standard encryption mechanism of the Flet framework).

5.2. The Operator adopts the following security measures:
- encryption of data at rest using AES-256;
- prevention of data transmission over unsecured communication channels;
- minimization of the scope of processed data.

5.3. Data transmission for the purpose of sending WOL packets occurs exclusively within the User's local network and is not transmitted to external servers.

## 6. Data Retention Period

6.1. Personal data is stored on the User's device until the User deletes the relevant record through the App interface (via swipe-to-delete gesture or by pressing the delete control).

6.2. Upon deletion of the App, all processed data is permanently removed from the User's device.

6.3. The Operator does not establish additional data retention periods, as data is not transmitted to the Operator's servers.

## 7. User Rights

7.1. The User has the right to:
- obtain information concerning the processing of their personal data;
- update, modify, or rectify personal data by editing information in the App;
- destroy personal data by deleting individual records or deleting the App;
- withdraw consent to the processing of personal data at any time by deleting the App;
- appeal the actions or inaction of the Operator to the authorized supervisory authority (Roskomnadzor).

## 8. Permissions

8.1. The App requires the following Android operating system permissions:
- **INTERNET**: required to send UDP broadcast packets over the local network;
- **ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE**: required to check network connectivity status.

8.2. These permissions are used exclusively for the functional purpose of the App.

## 9. Children's Privacy

9.1. The App is not intended for persons under the age of 14. The Operator does not intentionally collect personal data of minors.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

10.1. The Operator reserves the right to amend this Policy. The new version of the Policy takes effect upon its publication in the App, unless otherwise provided by the new version.

10.2. The current version of the Policy is always available at: https://github.com/IntelOut/wol_apk

## 11. Contact

11.1. All inquiries, suggestions, and requests relating to this Policy may be submitted through the Google Play Store listing.

11.2. The Operator reviews data subject requests within thirty (30) calendar days of receipt, in accordance with Article 20 of 152-FZ.
